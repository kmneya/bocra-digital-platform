About BOCRA

Profile

Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority (BOCRA) was established through the Communications Regulatory Authority Act, 2012 and began operations on 1 April 2013.

It regulates telecommunications, broadcasting, postal services, internet, and ICTs, creating a unified authority for Botswana’s communications sector.

Mission

To regulate the communications sector for the promotion of competition, innovation, consumer protection and universal access.

Vision

A connected and digitally driven society.
